Toy Poodle

The Poodle is known for superior intelligence and exceptional learning ability.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Lifespan: 12-14 Years
  • Habitat: Toy Poodles are normally found in the nearest person's lap or where ever food is being handed out.

Toy poodles are, as the name implies, pretty small dogs. This pup will stand no more than 9 inches tall and weigh less than 6 pounds. They can be quite fragile because of their small size, so you will need to pay extra attention to your dog while it is playing or moving around your home.
